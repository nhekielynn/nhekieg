ADOBO
Adobo is a dish that is usually made with meat (chicken, pork, or beef) marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic.Pour in soy sauce and vinegar and season with garlic powder, black pepper, and bay leaf.Return chicken to pan, increase heat to high, and bring to a boil. Cover until chicken is tender and cooked through.
